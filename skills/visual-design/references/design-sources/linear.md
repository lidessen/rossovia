# Linear

## Use for

Product-wide visual balance, dense professional interfaces, predictable action
placement, redesign timing, representative-view stress tests, and theme
generation.

## Decision lenses

- [A design reset](https://linear.app/now/a-design-reset) explains how local
  feature additions accumulate holistic design debt and why a concept direction
  may need to precede production.
- [How we redesigned the Linear UI](https://linear.app/now/how-we-redesigned-the-linear-ui)
  compares environment, appearance, and hierarchy across representative views
  and describes generating themes from base, accent, and contrast inputs.
- [A calmer interface for a product in motion](https://linear.app/now/behind-the-latest-design-refresh)
  shows later maintenance of predictable actions, consistent headers, and
  navigation that recedes so primary content can dominate.

## Transfer boundary

Recover holistic rebalancing, cross-view testing, density, alignment, and
theme-system decisions. Do not copy muted chrome, compactness, purple/gray
treatments, LCH parameters, or the “calm UI” appearance without equivalent
content, audience, and operating conditions. Periodic redesign is evidence from
one fast-moving product, not a universal schedule.
