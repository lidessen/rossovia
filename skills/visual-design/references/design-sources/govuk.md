# GOV.UK Design System

## Use for

Public-service clarity, trustworthy and accessible presentation, bounded reading
width, small-screen-first layout, and established content conventions.

## Decision lenses

- [Styles](https://design-system.service.gov.uk/styles/) separates page
  structure, typography, and visual elements within an established service
  language.
- [Layout](https://design-system.service.gov.uk/styles/layout/) demonstrates
  small-screen-first composition, readable line length, and grids chosen for
  understandable content rather than maximum occupancy.

## Transfer boundary

Recover user-need priority, restrained hierarchy, accessibility, and layout
discipline. Do not copy the government identity, typeface, colors, components,
or conventions into products whose trust relation and audience differ.
